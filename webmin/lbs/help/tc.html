Traffic Shaping
You can control here, the maximum network throughput that will be used by all the clients during a restoration. It's particularly useful to make sure that your network won't be saturated during a mass OS deployment.

Just specify the name of the related network interface, usually eth0, its theoretical throughput, and the allowed throughput.
Units such as 'mbit', 'kbit' (kilo/mega bits per second) or 'kbps', 'mbps' (kilo/mega bytes per second) should be specified.
